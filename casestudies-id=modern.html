Modern Blnd

A speculative product design project exploring corporate collaboration as a creative act.

01

BRIEF

Modern Blnd is a design exercise rooted in one question: what if the world's biggest companies stopped competing for the same consumer and started building for them together? Each product in this collection begins where two brand identities overlap and asks what could exist in that space; as a genuine response to how people live, move, and feel.

Technology-informed consumers don't experience brands in silos; they expect their tools, spaces, and objects to work in concert. Modern Blnd uses speculative cross-industry partnerships to imagine products that serve this reality, designed with the rigor of real-world constraints and the freedom of original thinking.

02

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Every product in this collection is built from a human-centered foundation informed by Behavioral Science, prioritizing how people actually interact with objects over how objects are expected to perform. The guiding principle across all three products is that good design should be emotionally resonant and technically sound in equal measure, accessible without being reductive, and innovative without being alienating.

03

DESIGN APPROACH

Each product was developed through a complete design process: identifying the cultural and functional gap a collaboration could credibly fill, developing concepts grounded in both brand identities, and modeling finished products in AutoCAD with hardware specifications, UI design, and manufacturing constraints factored in at every stage. This is speculative work built to real-world standards.

04

PROCESS

  • 01: Identify the gap a collaboration could credibly fill
  • 02: Develop concept grounded in both brand identities
  • 03: Design for user behavior, not just user preference
  • 04: Model in AutoCAD with real manufacturing constraints
  • 05: Refine UI, hardware specifications, and finish options
