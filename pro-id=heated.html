SPECULATIVE DESIGN

Heated

IKEA & Adobe — Biophilic Candle Warmer, Lamp & Incense Holder Designed for Everyday Creative Living

PARTNERSHIP IKEA & Adobe
CATEGORY Consumer Home Goods
DELIVERABLES Product Design, Strategy, Market Analysis
TOOLS AutoCAD, Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

01

THE OPPORTUNITY

The home has always been a space for rest. But rest and creativity aren't opposites; the right environment produces both. Research consistently shows that sensory cues shape cognitive state: ambient warmth shifts people toward intuitive, experiential thinking¹; pleasant fragrance improves mood and creative task performance³; nature-adjacent forms reduce mental fatigue and restore directed attention². Most home objects address one of these levers at a time. Heated addresses all three simultaneously.

Market Gap Identified: There's a missing design philosophy. One that treats atmosphere as something worth engineering.

02

THE COLLABORATION

IKEA has always believed that thoughtful design belongs in every home. Adobe has built its entire identity around the conditions that make creativity possible. Together, they share a premise: that your environment is not neutral. It either works for you or against you. Heated is what happens when democratic design meets creative infrastructure. A product that doesn't ask you to change your routine, it changes what your routine does to you.

03

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The mushroom form is structural. Inspired by mycelial networks, the silhouette reflects a biological truth: the most resilient systems are the ones where every element strengthens the others. In Heated, the warming system enhances fragrance distribution. The fragrance shapes mood. The mood shifts cognitive state. The biophilic form quietly signals safety and calm to a nervous system trained to read nature as rest.

This is what functional serenity means in practice. Research on nature-adjacent environments shows that even simulated exposure to organic forms and biophilic cues can improve convergent thinking and restore attentional resources depleted by screens, noise, and urban overstimulation². Environmental cues research further supports that dim, warm-toned spaces lower psychological guard and open people to broader associative thinking, the precondition for creative work⁴. Heated doesn't ask you to go outside. It brings the cognitive conditions of outside in.

04

WHY IKEA & ADOBE

IKEA's design language is built around the everyday; objects that fit life as it's actually lived, not as it's aspirationally imagined. Adobe's is built around the creative act itself, the tools, the space, the conditions that make original work possible. Heated sits at that intersection. It is not a luxury object performing accessibility. It is an accessible object performing at the level of luxury, precise, considered, and quietly transformative in the way that the best everyday things always are.

05

GO-TO-MARKET

Launch Strategy

Introduce Heated at Milan Design Week as a joint IKEA × Adobe installation, a fully staged environment demonstrating the product in its intended context, in a room. Visitors experience the warmth, the scent, the light, and the form together before they ever read a spec sheet.

Distribution

Available direct-to-consumer through IKEA's e-commerce platform and select flagship stores, with a limited Adobe Creative Cloud bundle, Heated as a physical companion to a digital creative subscription.

Premium positioning at a $149–179 price point, consistent with IKEA's accessible design ethos without undercutting the product's intentionality.

Market Approach

Lead with the science, not the aesthetic. Campaign creative draws on the body of environmental psychology research underpinning the design, translated into language that is felt rather than academic. Short-form content showing real creative environments: studios, home offices, reading corners. Influencer partnerships with designers, writers, and artists who already talk about their physical environment as part of their creative process.

Metrics

  • Units sold and repeat purchase rate across candle and incense consumables
  • Social engagement tracking environment and wellness adjacent content
  • Bundle conversion rate with Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Press coverage within design, wellness, and consumer technology verticals
