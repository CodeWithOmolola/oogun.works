01

Modern Blnd

Product Development & Design
December 2024

OVERVIEW

Modern Blnd is a speculative product design project exploring what happens when major companies stop competing and start co-creating; designing objects that meet a technology-informed generation exactly where they live. Each product was developed end-to-end, from concept through 3D modeling to go-to-market strategy, with human behavior as the starting point and cultural resonance as the standard.

AVAILABLE COLLABORATIONS

ALLUDE [GOOGLE & X] A pocket-sized AI device inspired by the Magic 8 Ball that bridges social media and intelligent search through an intuitive shake interface. Small enough for a keychain, Allude connects Google's knowledge base with X's social data. Shake twice for personalized responses, three times for information; with native integration across major platforms.
HEATED [IKEA & ADOBE] A mushroom-form lamp, candle warmer and incense holder that brings biophilic calm and smart ambiance control into everyday domestic life. Smart touch controls, an engineered ventilation system, and modular dual-function design make Heated as thoughtful as it is beautiful. A collaboration rooted in the belief that atmosphere is a design problem worth solving.
COMING SOON | SOULSIGHT MUSIC ♫ [IBM & BEATS] - A circular smart speaker and music application designed to make listening feel like an experience rather than a function. Real-time LED visualization responds to rhythm, beat, and mood, while 360° surround sound with a companion app experience currently in development.

02

GAP: Merchandising for Afromodernism

Strategic Analysis
August 2025

OVERVIEW

GAP built its identity on bridging gaps; generational, cultural, human. This case study examines how Afromodernist principles can close the distance between what consumers are demanding and what the industry continues to deliver: authentic cultural engagement, not appropriation. The proposal reimagines GAP's flagship spaces as sites of discovery, reciprocity, and living cultural narrative.
