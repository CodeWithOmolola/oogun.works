Ìri's Journal

Editorial Brand Development and Community Building

CLIENT Project Founder, Editor-in-Chief
TIMELINE Launched November 2023
LOCATION Chicago, IL
ROLE Brand Strategy, Editorial Direction, Community Management

01

THE VISION

Ìri's Journal is a creative publication and community platform showcasing diverse voices in art, culture, and storytelling. Founded to create space for emerging creatives who don't fit traditional editorial molds, the journal celebrates authentic expression over polished perfection.

The goal wasn't just to publish content, it was to build a community where contributors feel ownership and readers feel represented. Every issue is a collaboration between 15-20 writers, photographers, and artists working together to create something bigger than individual pieces.

02

Editorial Philosophy

Iri's Journal operates on the belief that everyone has stories worth telling, but not everyone has platforms to tell them. We actively seek contributors who are great storytellers but may lack formal journalism training, portfolio websites, or industry connections. Authenticity and perspective matter more than credentials.

Personal Essays

First-person narratives exploring identity, culture, mental health, and lived experience

Cultural Commentary

Analysis of trends, movements, and moments shaping contemporary culture

Photography & Visual Arts

Photo essays and visual storytelling from emerging photographers

Creative Writing

Poetry, short fiction, and experimental prose

03

BRANDING

Visual Identity

Clean, minimalist design that lets content shine. Typography-driven layouts with generous white space. Photography treated as equal partner to writing, not decoration. Each issue has cohesive aesthetic while allowing individual pieces to maintain unique voices.

Editorial Process

  • Open submission windows with transparent selection criteria
  • Collaborative editing focused on preserving writer voice while strengthening clarity
  • Multiple rounds of feedback between editors and contributors
  • Final approval from contributor before publication, we never publish without consent

Distribution

Digital-first publication distributed via website and Instagram. Each issue promoted through contributor networks, creating organic reach through authentic community sharing rather than paid advertising.
