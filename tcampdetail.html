STRATEGIC BRAND POSITIONING

Lola's Looks

Repositioning an Independent Hairstylist from Service Provider to Editorial Artist

CLIENT Independent Hairstylist
TIMELINE April - June 2023
LOCATION Chicago, IL
ROLE Creative Strategy, Art Direction, Photography

01

THE CHALLENGE

Independent hairstylists in Chicago face intense market saturation. With hundreds of stylists offering similar services at similar price points, differentiation becomes critical for survival.

The client needed to move beyond competing on price or convenience alone. The goal was to attract clients who valued artistry and were willing to pay premium rates for editorial-quality work—transforming the business from volume-based to value-based.

MARKET Saturated
COMPETITION High Volume
DIFFERENTIATION Low

02

THE STRATEGY

Reposition from service provider to editorial artist through a series of themed campaigns that showcase technical mastery and artistic vision.

Red Campaign (April 2023)

Launched during spring to capture attention with bold, saturated red tones. The campaign positioned the stylist as a color specialist capable of achieving editorial-quality results.

Satin Campaign (June 2023)

Timed for wedding season with sophisticated, luxurious styling. Demonstrated versatility while maintaining high-end positioning.

Blue Velvet (January 2024)

New year campaign emphasizing transformation and artistic boldness.

03

EXECUTION

Conceptual Photography

Elevated hair work to art form through editorial-style photography highlighting texture, color, and styling technique.

Platform Optimization

Created content adapted for Instagram feed, Stories, and Reels to maximize reach and engagement across formats.

Strategic Timing

Aligned campaign launches with seasonal booking patterns and cultural moments to maximize impact.

Visual Consistency

Maintained cohesive aesthetic across all touchpoints to build recognizable brand identity.

MEASURABLE IMPACT

57% Revenue Increase

May 2023 revenue compared to previous year, driven by higher-value bookings.

49% Revenue Increase

June 2023 sustained growth during peak wedding season.

60.5% Conversion Rate

Followers converting to actual clients—exceptionally high for service industry.

21% Premium Pricing

Service price increase accepted by market due to elevated positioning.

04

LONG-TERM IMPACT

The campaign's success extended far beyond immediate metrics. The editorial positioning attracted quality over quantity—clients who valued artistry and were willing to pay premium rates for exceptional work.

This strategic repositioning led to opportunities as Lead Hairstylist for music video productions (2021) and editorial photoshoots (2021-2023), establishing the client as a sought-after artist in Chicago's creative market rather than just another service provider.
