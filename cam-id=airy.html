STRATEGIC BRAND POSITIONING

Airy Hair by Ari

Audience Segmentation Strategy for Valentine's Day 2021

CLIENT Airy Hair by Ari
TIMELINE February 2021 (2-Week Campaign)
LOCATION Chicago, IL
ROLE Role Creative Strategy, Art Direction

01

THE CHALLENGE

Valentine's Day marketing typically defaults to romantic couples, alienating significant portions of potential customers. For a hair company, this traditional approach misses opportunities to engage single customers, friend groups, and people who celebrate self-love.

The client needed to increase February engagement and purchases without excluding non-romantic audiences. The goal was to make Valentine's Day feel inclusive and relevant to all customers, regardless of relationship status.

02

THE STRATEGY

Rather than one generic Valentine's campaign, we created three distinct narratives targeting different customer segments, each with its own messaging, visual identity, and emotional appeal.

Each segment received distinct visual and color treatment while maintaining cohesive brand aesthetic. Self-Love used bold individual portraits, Galantine’s featured group energy, Romantic Valentine's emphasized elegance and intimacy.

Self-Love Series

Targeted single customers and people prioritizing self-care.
Messaging: "You deserve to feel beautiful for yourself." Emphasized personal confidence and self-investment.

Galantine’s Day

Targeted friend groups celebrating together.
Messaging: "Celebrate your best friends," by creating group bundles incentive.

Romantic Valentine's

Targeted couples preparing for date night.
Messaging: Reinforcing hair maintenance as part of Valentine's date night preparation ritual.

03

EXECUTION

Staggered Release Schedule

Released one segment every 3-4 days over two weeks to maintain momentum and reach different audiences

Tailored Messaging

Each post spoke directly to its target segment's emotional needs and purchasing motivations

Platform Optimization

Instagram Stories for behind-the-scenes content, Feed for hero imagery, Reels for process videos

MEASURABLE IMPACT

29.5% Follower Growth

In Just Two Weeks

3X Engagement Rate

Compared to Typical Posts

476% Account Reach

Across platform content prior reach averaged 177 to 844

04

CAMPAIGN TAKEAWAY

The segmentation approach proved that holiday marketing doesn't need to be one-size-fits-all. By creating multiple entry points, we captured customers who would have ignored a traditional romantic Valentine's campaign.

Most importantly, the Self-Love and Galentine's segments outperformed the romantic track in engagements, validating the decision to expand beyond traditional Valentine's messaging.
