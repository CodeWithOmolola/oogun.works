APPROACH

I'm a creative strategist specializing in culturally-informed brand strategies that drive measurable growth.. With a background in Behavioral Science and 5+ years at the intersection of culture, and consumer behavior, I bridge strategic thinking with creative execution.

I believe modern marketing must be rooted in the sentiments of Afromodernism, not just its visual aesthetics, but in genuinely engaging the communities and cultural contexts that give those aesthetics meaning. Effective consumer engagement requires understanding the entirety of people within their contexts, not surface-level trend adoption.

My approach centers on one belief: the best solutions come from acquiring whatever skills the problem demands. When challenges require new capabilities, whether learning AutoCAD to render product concepts, building automation systems to streamline workflows, or founding a publication to explore cultural discourse, I develop them. This methodology has enabled me to drive 57% revenue increases through campaign strategy, design product innovations that explore untapped market opportunities, and build systems that elevate team performance. I don't fit neatly into traditional role definitions because the problems worth solving rarely do.
