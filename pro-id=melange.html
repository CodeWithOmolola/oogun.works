SPECULATIVE DESIGN

Mélange

Dairy-Free & Naturally Elevated — Premium Non-Dairy Ice Cream

CATEGORY Consumer Packaged Goods / Food & Beverage
DELIVERABLES Brand Identity, Packaging Design, Flavor Development, Market Positioning
TOOLS Adobe Creative Suite, Affinity Designer, Product Mockups

01

OVERVIEW

Born from a desire to reimagine both non-dairy ice cream and the humble melon, Mélange celebrates the art of thoughtful combination. Drawing inspiration from French culinary traditions and global flavor profiles, we craft sophisticated frozen desserts that elevate familiar melons through careful blending with complementary herbs and spices.
Mélange proves that non-dairy doesn't mean compromise, it means creativity. Each flavor is a study in balance, texture, and unexpected harmony.

Understanding Mélange

Most non-dairy ice cream brands take one of two approaches, replicate traditional dairy flavors (vanilla, chocolate, mint chip) using alternative bases, or go aggressively "weird" with flavors that feel forced (lavender-charcoal-CBD-whatever).

Melon, despite being universally loved, is criminally underutilized in frozen desserts. It's seen as "basic" or relegated to fruit salads, never the star. Mélange positions melon as premium ingredient worthy of the same treatment as exotic fruits, pairing it with herbs and spices that highlight its natural sweetness and complexity.

02

STRATEGIC FOUNDATION

Target Audience

Food-forward consumers (25-58) who view dietary preferences as lifestyle, not limitation. They follow food media, experiment with recipes, and appreciate when brands respect their intelligence. Non-dairy by choice or necessity, but unwilling to settle for "good enough for vegan.”

Brand Positioning

"Where French technique meets global palate." Mélange sits between boutique artisan brands and mass-market non-dairy options. Premium ingredients, thoughtful combinations, accessible luxury.

Key Differentiators

Melon-forward flavor profile (unexpected hero ingredient)

Culinary-driven combinations grounded in real food traditions

Organic oat & coconut cream base

Clean ingredient list: no gums, no fillers, no fake flavors

03

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Mélange (French for "blend/mixture") is about harmony. We don't just throw trending ingredients together, we study flavor pairings, test ratios, and craft combinations that make sense both culinarily and culturally. Watermelon + rose water works because Middle Eastern cuisine has paired them for centuries. Cantaloupe + black pepper works because savory-sweet contrast is fundamental to Italian cooking. We're not inventing; we're honoring traditions and applying them to frozen dessert format.

HONEYDEW BASIL

  • Inspiration: Italian tradition of pairing melon with prosciutto and herbs. Honeydew's mild sweetness meets herbaceous, slightly peppery basil.
  • Base: Honeydew melon puree, organic oat & coconut cream, fresh basil infusion, organic cane sugar, natural flavors, locust bean gum, guar gum, sea salt

CANTALOUPE BLACK PEPPER

  • Inspiration: Classic Italian antipasto where cantaloupe's sweetness is enhanced by cracked black pepper. The pepper brings forward tropical notes in the melon while adding gentle heat.
  • Base: Cantaloupe puree, organic oat & coconut cream, freshly cracked black pepper, organic cane sugar, locust bean gum, guar gum, sea salt

WATERMELON ROSE

  • Inspiration: Middle Eastern and Persian cuisine where rose water elevates watermelon's natural floral qualities. Light, refreshing, romantic.
  • Base: Watermelon juice, organic oat & coconut cream, rose water, organic cane sugar, natural flavors, locust bean gum, guar gum, sea salt

PERSIAN MELON CARDAMOM

  • Inspiration: Persian and French desserts where cardamom's warm, citrusy spice complements delicate melons. Complex, aromatic.
  • Base: Persian melon puree, organic oat & coconut cream, fresh cardamon, organic cane sugar, natural flavors, locust bean gum, guar gum, sea salt

Visual Identity

  • Packaging features custom line-art illustrations of each melon variety, emphasizing the fruit's natural beauty. The drawings are botanical and artistic.

    Color palette is derived from the melons themselves: warm cantaloupe orange, soft watermelon pink, gentle honeydew green, golden Persian melon yellow. Each flavor has its own color story, but they work as cohesive family.

Typography & Layout

  • Clean, serif typeface (inspired by French patisserie signage) for brand name. Sans-serif for flavor names and nutritional information.

    Layout balances functional clarity and beauty for display on counter, and practical enough to read ingredient list quickly.

Packaging System

  • Pint containers (16 oz) with kraft paper shopping bags featuring repeated brand mark. Lid design includes flavor story, brief explanation of why these ingredients pair well. This transforms packaging into educational moment, building brand authority around flavor knowledge.

04

GO-TO-MARKET

Launch Strategy

Summer launch (peak melon season) via farmers market pop-ups and local specialty grocers in food-forward cities (Portland, Austin, Brooklyn, SF). Build brand story through sampling and direct consumer feedback before scaling production.

Distribution

Year 1: Independent grocers, co-ops, upscale markets (Erewhon, New Seasons, Central Market).

Year 2: Whole Foods regional test.

Year 3: National Whole Foods + Target premium frozen section. DTC subscription model for superfans.

Market Approach

Content focused on flavor education: Instagram posts explaining why basil + honeydew works, blog recipes using Mélange in creative ways including, affogato with cantaloupe black pepper, watermelon rose float with prosecco. Partner with food bloggers and recipe developers. Position as ingredient, not just dessert.

Pricing

  • Premium positioning at $6.99-7.99 per pint vs mass-market $4.99 & boutique $9.99
  • Pricing reflects organic ingredients and small-batch production
  • Seasonal limited editions at $8.99 to test new flavors
