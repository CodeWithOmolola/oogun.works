02

Lola's Looks

Strategic Brand Positioning
April - June 2023

CHALLENGE

Independent hairstylists needed to differentiate in the saturated Chicago market and attract clients seeking bold, editorial-quality color work.

STRATEGY

Repositioned client from service provider to editorial artist through themed campaign series showcasing technical skill and artistic range. Created Red Campaign (April) for spring attention-capture and Satin Campaign (June) for sophisticated summer wedding season positioning.

03

Airy Hair by Ari

Audience Segmentation Strategy
Valentine's Day 2021


CHALLENGE

Hair extension brand needed Valentine's content beyond generic couple imagery to reach underserved markets and drive seasonal sales.

STRATEGY

Developed three-pillar content series reframing Valentine's around different expressions of love: Lover Girl (traditional couples), Galentine's Day (friendship), and Self-Love (self-care). Each theme featured cohesive visual identity with strategic posting schedule.

29.5% Follower Growth
476% Increase in Reach
